Oven Roasted Potatoes

Description

Roasted potatoes in the oven that are easy to make. Soak them, boil them with baking soda, put them in flavorful oils, and pop them in the oven.

Ingredients

  • Russet Potatoes
  • Baking Soda
  • Flavorful Oils
  • Water

Steps

  1. Chop potatoes into big chunks and let them soak in the water.
  2. Add potatoes to a pot of water with baking soda. Stop when a fork can pierce it, but still provide some resistance.
  3. Strain it and let it cool until there is no more steam. Transfer it to a big bowl.
  4. Add flavorful oils here and agitate the potatoes until coated.
  5. Transfer to a baking sheet. Put the oven at 450F and let them cook for 20 min.
  6. Flip potatoes, and let it roast for 25 minutes.
