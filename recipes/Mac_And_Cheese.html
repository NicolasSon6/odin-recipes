Macaroni and Cheese

Description

Mac and Cheese with Panko, for crispy, cheesy goodness.

Ingredients

  • Macaroni Shells
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Cheeses of your choice

    • Steps

    1. Cook your macaroni as listed in the box, but do not overcook it. Drain it when done.
    2. Melt butter in pan. When melted, add flour and whisk it until smooth with no lumps.
    3. Add milk and stir well until it boils. Milk and butter will separate, stir until combined.
    4. When boiled, remove it off the heat and bring it to a simmer. Stir until thickened.
    5. Reduce heat to low, and add your cheeses. Stir until melted and smooth. Combine macaroni shells with the cheese in a baking pan.
    6. When combining, leave some cheese to put on top.
    7. Combine butter and oil in a sauce pan and stir until melted.
    8. Add panko and stir well until lightly toasted. Add it to the top of the macaroni and cheese.
    9. Transfer to oven at 350F for 15 minutes.
