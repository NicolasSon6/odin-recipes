One Pot Creamy Pasta

Description

Easy to make pasta. Covers meat, veggies, and carbs.

Ingredients

  • Pasta of your choice
  • Cream Cheese
  • Chicken
  • Chicken Broth
  • Butter
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Cut chicken to 1-inch pieces and add butter to skillet. Add chicken on medium high heat until medium brown.
  2. Mince and add garlic then saute for a minute.
  3. Add uncooked pasta and chicken broth. Stir to dissolve any browning bits. Place a lid until boil on medium high heat.
  4. Stir and put on low heat. Stir four times every two minutes, or until the pasta is tender.
  5. Add milk and cream cheese and stir over medium heat until all combined.
